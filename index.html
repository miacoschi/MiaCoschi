I'm a graphic designer with a visual arts background, specializing in critical studies, where concept is highly valued. I place importance on research and symbolism, even in the simplest of designs. I appreciate expressive and playful designs, often integrating my own artistic touch, with a preference for bringing physical media into the digital realm. While my work is expressive, I also appreciate simplicity, finding that less is often more. I have developed an adaptable style that can be easily curated to a brand’s needs.
